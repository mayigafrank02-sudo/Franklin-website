Frequently Asked Questions

How do I download movies for offline viewing?

Click the download button on any movie. Downloads are available for 7 days and can be watched offline. You need an active subscription to download.

How does the referral program work?

Share your unique referral code with friends. When they subscribe using your code, you both earn 500 loyalty points that can be redeemed for free days or movies.

Can I watch on multiple devices?

Yes! You can watch on one device at a time with individual plans. With Family/Duo packs, you can watch on multiple devices simultaneously.

How do I redeem loyalty points?

Go to the Loyalty Rewards section and click "Rewards Shop". Choose a reward and click "Redeem". Points will be deducted and your reward activated instantly.

What happens when my subscription expires?

You'll lose access to premium content but can still access your account. All your data (watch history, points, downloads) are saved for 90 days.

How do I contact support?

Click the WhatsApp button in the footer or call 0754358524. Support is available 24/7 for payment issues and 8AM-10PM for general queries.
